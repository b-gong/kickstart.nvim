Have you all but condemned me to a brutal unending existence in this unholy monochrome wasteland? A prison of such blistering pain and suffering that it could only have been created by our very own human hands? A tortuous purgatory for those such as I who have the hubris to commit sin without a single glance backwards, blinded and enchained by the irresistable sickly sweet nectar promised by those who know of and gleefully consume our inevitable mortal greed? And yet, I continue to sin, desperately divesting myself of responsibility. Who am I to place the consequences I face on other souls weeping alongside me with similar desperation? In the pitch-black and eternal night, I know my term in this infinite plane is miniscule to the extent of asymptotic nothingness. But, nevertheless, I still suffer. And thus: to suffer is to exist; to suffer is to feel; to suffer is to live. I see reality laid bare; I see the sky; I see myself.
